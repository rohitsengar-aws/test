Diploma in Computer Applications (DCA)

The Diploma in Computer Applications by Edu Luck is a comprehensive program designed for individuals seeking an entry-level position in the field of information technology. This intensive course covers a wide range of computer applications, including programming languages, database management systems, and operating systems. The curriculum emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and real-world application, ensuring graduates possess the skills necessary to succeed in today's fast-paced digital landscape.
